Nepal

Nepal is a landlocked country in South Asia, known for its stunning Himalayan mountains, including Mount Everest,
the world's tallest peak. Rich in culture and history, it is home to ancient temples, diverse wildlife, and a welcoming population.

Japan

Japan is an island nation in East Asia, famous for its technological advancements, traditional culture, and breathtaking
landscapes. Known as the "Land of the Rising Sun," it offers vibrant cities, historic temples, and a unique blend of modernity and tradition.

Peru

Peru, located in South America, is renowned for its rich history, including the ancient Inca civilization. It is home to
iconic sites like Machu Picchu, diverse ecosystems, and vibrant cultural traditions influenced by indigenous and Spanish heritage.

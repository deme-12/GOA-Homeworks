About Peru

Peru Location: Western South America Capital: Lima Language: Spanish (official), Quechua, Aymara Key Features: Known for the Andes Mountains, the Amazon rainforest, and the ancient Inca city of Machu Picchu. Rich in biodiversity, with varied climates ranging from coastal deserts to tropical forests. Famous for its vibrant culture, blending Indigenous, Spanish, and African influences, with a renowned culinary scene featuring dishes like ceviche.

About Japan

Japan Location: East Asia, an archipelago in the Pacific Ocean Capital: Tokyo Language: Japanese Key Features: Comprises four main islands: Honshu, Hokkaido, Kyushu, and Shikoku. Known for technological innovation, ancient traditions like tea ceremonies, and iconic sites such as Mount Fuji and Kyoto’s temples. A highly developed country with a rich history, blending modern cities with deep cultural heritage.

About Nepal

Nepal Location: South Asia, landlocked between India and China Capital: Kathmandu Language: Nepali (official) Key Features: Home to the Himalayas, including Mount Everest, the world’s highest peak. Known for its spiritual heritage, being the birthplace of Gautama Buddha. Diverse geography ranging from plains in the south to mountainous regions in the north.
